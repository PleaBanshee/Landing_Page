ABOUT ME

Hello there! I am Llewellyn Anthony

I am an aspiring software engineer. I'm 22 years old. Ever since I went to college I knew this would be my dream job one day. I always wanted to develop desktop apps with C#, but after doing some research I've realised thre are a lot more opportunities availible in the Web Development area. Now I'm trying to sharpen my web skills and become a full-stack web developer. I would also like to write some web apps in the future.

Characteristics

I'm rather shy, on the verge of being anti-social. These days I spend a lot of time improving my programming skills and reading on Computer Science topics to stay up to date. I like watching movies and cartoons in my free time, as well as jogging a 5k late in the afternoons. My next interests is becoming a master developer in Python and JavaScript. As you can see, I'm a coding junkie and my life revolves around it. Writing code is one of the most satisfying things I've ever experienced in my life, there's nothing as great as solving a problem and know you've done well...

Future Plans

At the moment I'm rather uncertain where my future is steering me. There are so much things I'd like to learn before taking a full-time job. I'd like to take a course on network security/hacking. I'd also like to become more familiar with a Linux distribution, because most developers use different distributions of Linux to develop and test software. I'm currently a final year BSc-IT student at the NWU, I'd also like to do my Honours. I'd like to work for a smaller company after graduating, just to gain some experience and getting the hang of collaborating and developing software with others. Then who knows, I might do some freelancing or even start my own company...
